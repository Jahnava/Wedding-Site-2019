How We Met

Jahnava's Story


Scott and I met in the piano room of a little venue downtown. Scott was playing solo piano and enjoying his night. I ventured by and interrupted his song by nonchalantly striking a "C" chord. Scott didn't seem to mind the intrusion and playfully said "now you have to sit down and play a song with me". Scott and I played "Heart and Soul" and talked for a while. Thereafter, we became friends and had random rendezvous including the beach, springs, furniture assembly, and cleaning my apartment fans at three in the morning. Scott gave me this adorable note that said "Will you go out with me, Yes or no?”. I couldn’t picture myself without him, so I checked "yes". Now, we're engaged to be friends for a lifetime!
