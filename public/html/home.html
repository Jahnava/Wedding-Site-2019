Our Story

Scott and Jahnava met in the piano room of a little venue downtown. Scott was playing solo piano and enjoying his night. Jahnava ventured by and interrupted his song by nonchalantly striking a "C" chord. Scott didn't mind the intrusion and playfully said "now you have to sit down and play a song with me". Scott and Jahnava played "Heart and Soul" and talked for a while. Thereafter, they became friends and had many rendezvous including the beach, springs, furniture assembly, music, painting with fireworks, and cleaning Jahnava's apartment fans at three in the morning. One day Scott decided to give Jahnava this adorable note that said "Will you go out with me, Yes or no?”. She couldn’t picture herself without him, so she checked "yes". Now, They've been engaged for years, have a house together, and two children. The stars have aligned perfectly for their wedding to take place on Sep 29th, so they're finally celebrating their union with an official ceremony.
